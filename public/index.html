About Our Society

Krishnanagar Society is a peaceful and well-maintained residential area located in the heart of our city. We believe in a digital-first approach for better transparency, faster complaint redressal, and building a strong community. Our cloud-based complaint management portal allows residents to easily file issues and track them in real-time. Cleanliness, safety, and prompt service are the pillars of our society.
